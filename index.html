About Us

Colonial Gardens is located in central Maine, where residents can enjoy the beautiful landscapes of the vacation state. Set in a quiet, respectful, and safe community incorporated in a country setting, yet still easy access to local entities.

In 1965, Colonial Gardens was established by Conrad Goddard and Harry Curtis based on the idea of assisting the needs of the clients. The company started as a small eleven sites community. To commemorate this special occasion, that same year a street was named after Conrad and Cheryl’s newborn daughter Cynthia.

In 1968, Conrad and Cheryl Goddard acquired Mr. Curtis’ involvement in the community, more property was purchased and over the years a total of 94 lots have been developed.

As a family owned business, the Goddard family has developed a commitment and great pride in community, also providing a hospitable environment for their residents.

We thank you for visiting our website. If you have any questions or want to receive information please contact us at (207) 353-2810 or email us at info@colonialgardensinc.com.

Colonial Gardens provides the following commodities:

  • Wooded lot for shade and privacy
  • Landscaped sites and common areas
  • 6" reinforced concrete pads
  • Paved two-vehicle driveway and walkway
  • Public water and wastewater services
  • Maintained streets
  • Weekly trash and yard waste removal
  • To maintain a quiet community, no dogs are permitted

Prime location minutes from Lewiston-Auburn, Brunswick, and Freeport where the famous L.L. Bean is located.
