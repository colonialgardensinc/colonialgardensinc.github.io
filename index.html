About Us

Colonial Gardens is located in central Maine, where residents can enjoy the beautiful landscapes of the vacation state. It is set in a quiet, respectful, and safe community with a country feel, while still offering convenient access to local destinations.

In 1965, Colonial Gardens was established by Conrad Goddard and Harry Curtis based on the idea of assisting the needs of clients. The company started as a small eleven-site community. To commemorate this special occasion, that same year a street was named after Conrad and Cheryl’s newborn daughter, Cynthia.

In 1968, Conrad and Cheryl Goddard acquired Mr. Curtis’ involvement in the community. More property was purchased, and over the years a total of 94 lots have been developed.

As a family-owned business, the Goddard family has developed a deep commitment to the community and takes pride in providing a hospitable environment for residents.

We thank you for visiting our website. If you have any questions or would like more information, please contact us at (207) 353-2810 or email us at info@colonialgardensinc.com.
